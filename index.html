About the Event

LatinaSTEMpact is a one-day celebration dedicated to uplifting and recognizing Latina women in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. This event brings together students, professionals, educators, and allies to share stories, showcase research, and build lasting mentorship networks. Born out of the need for greater representation and visibility in STEM fields, LatinaSTEMpact creates a vibrant space where culture and innovation thrive side by side.

If you're a high school student curious about coding or a seasoned researcher looking to give back, this event is for you.

What to Expect

Igniting Futures, Empowering Voices

  • Inspirational Keynotes: Hear from pioneering Latina leaders who are breaking barriers and shaping the future of STEM.
  • Interactive Workshops: Dive into hands-on sessions covering coding, AI, data science, and more, tailored for all skill levels.
  • Networking & Mentorship: Connect with fellow students, seasoned professionals, and potential mentors to build lasting relationships.
  • Career Pathways Panel: Gain insights into diverse career opportunities in STEM and learn how to navigate your professional journey.
  • Community Building: Join a supportive network dedicated to celebrating culture, fostering innovation, and championing Latina excellence in STEM.
